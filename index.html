About us
We at Skill Station, empowering students to achieve their career goals by our unique education and skill training programmes. Skill station is founded by a group of like-minded educators who are passionate about teaching and mentorship. We aim to inculcate intrinsic motivation among the students through our research based education methods and peer-to-peer mentorship programmes to cultivate leadership qualities at a very early stage in their career.
